HackNotts begins at 11am on the 11th of December with an opening ceremony, before the hacking starts at exactly 12 noon, going on for a full 24 hours.

Throughout the 24 hour hacking time, we'll have workshops, games, and other events to keep you entertained. Some of these are run by our sponsors - make sure to go to them, they're the ones giving us money lol.

Once hacking ends, there'll be a closing ceremony, including prizes for all our winners!
