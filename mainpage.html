HackNotts 2025

HackNotts is an annual hackathon held by HackSoc at the University of Nottingham. It's a two-day overnight hackathon for all skill levels where you can engage in friendly competition to create an exciting and new project within a 24 hour period, with a chance to win some of our amazing prizes!
